JEE - Preparation Process

At Vethathiri Academy, we offer structured and student-friendly JEE coaching programs to prepare aspirants for JEE Main and JEE Advanced. Each course is designed to build strong conceptual clarity in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, with rigorous practice, mock tests, and performance tracking to ensure results.

Crash Course for JEE

A short-term, high-intensity course ideal for last-stage preparation. This course is tailored for quick revision, formula recap, past year questions, and mock exams.

  • Full-length mock tests with answer discussion
  • Revision of all important concepts
  • Doubt-clearing classes

Foundation Courses

This program helps younger students build a strong base in Physics, Chemistry, and Maths, making them ready for the JEE level early.

  • NCERT-based concept teaching
  • Logical reasoning and aptitude training
  • Strong base for JEE and board preparation
