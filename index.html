Vision - Vethathiri Academy

Vethathiri Academy envisions a peaceful, harmonious world where individuals lead conscious and purposeful lives rooted in spiritual awareness, moral values, and universal brotherhood. Guided by the timeless teachings of Shri Vethathiri Maharishi, the academy aspires to be a global center for inner transformation—nurturing the body, mind, and soul through the practice of Simplified Kundalini Yoga, meditation, and introspection.

Mission - Vethathiri Academy

The mission of Vethathiri Academy is to empower individuals with practical tools for holistic living through scientifically structured courses in yoga, meditation, and mind training. By integrating ancient wisdom with modern understanding, the academy aims to help learners achieve physical health, mental peace, and spiritual elevation. It is dedicated to building a value-based society by nurturing discipline, emotional intelligence, self-confidence, and environmental consciousness.
